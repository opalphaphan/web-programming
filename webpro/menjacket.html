    Off-White™ c/o Chicago Bulls Blue Varsity


        Off-White™ celebrates Virgil Abloh’s passion for basketball and his hometown by joining forces with the legendary Chicago Bulls to launch a capsule collection available exclusively on Off-White’s site and CHRIS. Debuting on the day the Bulls play the Detroit Pistons in Paris in January 2023 – a momentous event in the sporting calendar – it presents collegiate styles underpinned by an irreverent spirit.


    Part of the proceeds will be donated to the Virgil Abloh Foundation.


        Off-White™ is known for injecting classic silhouettes with the unexpected. Available in two colourways, the steel grey and navy version of this sporty patchwork jacket is emblazoned with the Chicago Bulls’ mascot, Benny the Bull, and a “Bulls” motif on the back. Double quotation marks pay subtle homage to Off-White’s late founder Virgil Abloh’s enduring signal to question and reimagine everything. Meanwhile, the red-and-cream iteration plays on the idea of debut seasons in sport and fashion – the Bulls’ in 1966 and Off-White’s in 2013. Uniting the designs is the brand’s iconic Diag-stripe print at the sleeve.


    This release is part of CHRIS BEAT – our new curated programme introducing limited-edition releases in collaboration with the most progressive voices in fashion and culture today.

        Since its launch in 2012, Off-White has revolutionised the contemporary style world with its unique hybrid of streetwear and high fashion. Spearheaded by esteemed American designer and creative multi-hyphenate Virgil Abloh, the Milan-based label continues to produce cutting edge designs that challenge the traditions of the fashion industry – think unconventional cuts, bold graphics and tongue-in-cheek slogans. Off-White is perhaps best known for its industrial yellow accessories, cross logo and iconic zip tag – hallmarks that are highly sought after by streetwear enthusiasts and luxury fashion devotees alike.
