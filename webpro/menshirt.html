The “Bulls” motif’s double quotation marks are a nod to Abloh’s enduring signal to question and reimagine everything.

        Since its launch in 2012, Off-White has revolutionised the contemporary style world with its unique hybrid of streetwear and high fashion. Off-White is perhaps best known for its industrial yellow accessories, cross logo and iconic zip tag – hallmarks that are highly sought after by streetwear enthusiasts and luxury fashion devotees alike.
